About More

Vision Our vision at Urban Baithak is to be the epitome of culinary excellence, where global flavors harmonize to create a tapestry of tastes that transcends boundaries. We strive to be the ultimate destination that not only satisfies appetites but also nurtures a sense of community and cultural appreciation. Through our innovative approach and commitment to authenticity, we aim to become a beacon of culinary artistry that resonates with both local and international palates.

Mission At Urban Baithak, our mission is to craft an unparalleled dining experience by bringing together the diverse expertise of Naveen Gahlaut and Harvinder Singh. Drawing inspiration from their collective experiences across the globe, we endeavor to present a menu that showcases a symphony of flavors, blending traditional recipes with contemporary techniques. Our dedication to using only the finest ingredients, coupled with a warm and inviting atmosphere, is geared towards creating cherished memories for our patrons. Through continuous innovation, cultural exchange, and a passion for culinary mastery, we aspire to redefine the boundaries of taste and hospitality.
