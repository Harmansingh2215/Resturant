About Us

Welcome to Urban Baithak

A culinary haven where flavors from around the world come together to create a truly exceptional dining experience. Helmed by visionary owners Naveen Gahlaut and Harvinder Singh Ramgarhia, who collectively boast over 35 years of expertise in the culinary world, Urban Baithak is a testament to their extensive journeys across continents. With Naveen's mastery as a Chef and General Manager, spanning iconic destinations such as Switzerland, U.K, U.S.A, Canada, and India, and his ownership of a successful cafe in India, coupled with Harvinder Singh's 15-year culinary odyssey through Australia, India, and Canada, Urban Baithak promises a symphony of tastes that celebrate diversity and innovation. Prepare to embark on a gastronomic adventure that bridges cultures and redefines the art of dining.

Vision Urban Baithak

Our vision at Urban Baithak is to be the epitome of culinary excellence, where global flavors harmonize to create a tapestry of tastes that transcends boundaries. We strive to be the ultimate destination that not only satisfies appetites but also nurtures a sense of community and cultural appreciation. Through our innovative approach and commitment to authenticity, we aim to become a beacon of culinary artistry that resonates with both local and international palates.

Mission Urban Baithak

At Urban Baithak, our mission is to craft an unparalleled dining experience by bringing together the diverse expertise of Naveen Gahlaut and Harvinder Singh. Drawing inspiration from their collective experiences across the globe, we endeavor to present a menu that showcases a symphony of flavors, blending traditional recipes with contemporary techniques. Our dedication to using only the finest ingredients, coupled with a warm and inviting atmosphere, is geared towards creating cherished memories for our patrons. Through continuous innovation, cultural exchange, and a passion for culinary mastery, we aspire to redefine the boundaries of taste and hospitality.
