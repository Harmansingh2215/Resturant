Urban Baithak

History The captivating table of Urban Baithak weaves together the aspirations and journeys of four culinary aficionados – Naveen Gahlaut, Harvinder Singh, Kanchan, and Megha – each contributing their unique experiences to the rich tapestry of this culinary haven.Naveen Gahlaut, with a distinguished career spanning over two decades as a Chef and General Manager, crisscrossed the globe, leaving his culinary imprints in Switzerland, the U.K, the U.S.A, Canada, and his homeland, India. His expertise was not just limited to the culinary realm; Naveen's ownership of a thriving café in India attested to his entrepreneurial spirit.

In 2023, the dream materialized into reality as Urban Baithak opened its doors, a result of the collective passion, expertise, and dedication of these four visionaries. The restaurant's name, "Urban Baithak," spoke of its essence – a modern gathering place where cultures mingled and flavors harmonized.The menu was meticulously curated, a testament to the fusion of global experiences and regional authenticity.

Visions of Urban Baithak is to be the epitome of culinary excellence, where global flavors harmonize to create a tapestry of tastes that transcends boundaries. We strive to be the ultimate destination that not only satisfies appetites but also nurtures a sense of community and cultural appreciation. Through our innovative approach and commitment to authenticity, we aim to become a beacon of culinary artistry that resonates with both local and international palates.

Harvinder Singh's culinary voyage unfolded over 15 years, touching kitchens in Australia, India, and Canada. His flair for blending tradition with contemporary innovation perfectly complemented Naveen's global perspective. The two crossed paths, igniting a shared dream – Urban Baithak, a place where flavors united, and cultures conversed.The result is not just a restaurant but a living testament to the love of food, hospitality, and the spirit of unity.

Mission At Urban Baithak, our mission is to craft an unparalleled dining experience by bringing together the diverse expertise of Naveen Gahlaut and Harvinder Singh. Drawing inspiration from their collective experiences across the globe, we endeavor to present a menu that showcases a symphony of flavors. Our dedication to using only the finest ingredients, coupled with a warm and inviting atmosphere, is geared towards creating cherished memories for our patrons. Through continuous innovation, cultural exchange, and a passion for culinary mastery, we aspire to redefine the boundaries of taste and hospitality.

Over time, Urban Baithak emerged not just as a restaurant but as a cultural institution, earning accolades for its exceptional cuisine and inviting ambiance. The partnership of Naveen, Harvinder, Kanchan, and Megha had kindled a culinary revolution, redefining dining experiences and transcending geographical boundaries.Today, Urban Baithak stands as a testament to the power of dreams, collaboration, and unwavering dedication. Its story is a tribute to the culinary arts and the belief that when hearts align.
